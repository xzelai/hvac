How it works (simple)
We add a missed-call layer on top of your existing phone setup. No platform switch.
Step 1 — Connect missed calls

If your team doesn’t answer, the call routes to the intake layer. Your main number stays the same.

Step 2 — Capture the lead

Short HVAC intake: name, address/zip, issue (AC/heat/maintenance), and urgency. No diagnosing. No pricing.

Step 3 — Text the summary to your team

We text (and/or email) the missed-call summary to the owner/dispatcher/team—whatever number(s) you choose.
